About artist

Welcome to my site!

My name is Aljona Voronova, I am 40 years old and I live in Austria, Zell am See. I love painting on glass and have been doing this since 2003. I use German paints and contours for glass by the company "Marabu". I am also doing wall paintings and ornaments. One of my specialities as an artist is the design, the painting and the supervision of the manufacturing of unique chandeliers of all sizes and configurations. I am doing also interior design and home decoration.

Contact

On the technique of glass painting: Some patterns on the glass may be applied in a horizontal position, however others can be applied also on a vertical window, the size is unlimited. The enamel I use exists in two different styles: visible in any light and not transparent.
